# Product Features

This file describes the intended feature areas. It does not define demo scope. See `../demo/scope.md` for implementation priority.

## Habit and goal definition

Users can define:

- Title
- Motivation
- Schedule or frequency
- Minimum completion rule
- Start date
- Privacy level
- Accountability mode
- Optional evidence type

## Accountability partners

Users can:

- Invite a partner
- Accept or decline an invitation
- See shared commitments
- Encourage each other
- Acknowledge check-ins
- Respond to missed commitments
- End or pause the relationship

Future versions may support automatic partner matching.

## Check-ins

A check-in records the outcome of a commitment period.

Possible states:

- Completed
- Partially completed
- Missed
- Skipped for an accepted reason
- Pending

A check-in may contain:

- Short note
- Photo or other evidence
- Duration
- Quantity
- Mood or difficulty
- Partner response

## Daily commitment view

The user should see:

- What is due today
- Current status
- The smallest next action
- Partner activity
- Recent encouragement
- Progress summary

## Social feed

A future feed may show relevant commitment activity from partners, groups, and challenges.

The feed should prioritize actionable events over passive content.

## Groups and challenges

Users may participate in:

- Small accountability groups
- Time-limited challenges
- Shared habits
- Local or community activities
- Team goals

## Gamification

Potential mechanics include:

- Points
- Levels
- Reliability score
- Badges
- Streaks
- Recovery streaks
- Team milestones
- Challenge rankings

Gamification must reward truthful action and healthy recovery.

## Rewards and benefits

Users may earn:

- In-app recognition
- Partner rewards
- Discounts
- Community benefits
- Access to challenges
- Real-world perks

Real reward fulfillment is outside the first demo.

## Progress analytics

Potential insights include:

- Completion rate
- Current streak
- Best streak
- Recovery time
- Most successful days
- Partner response patterns
- Habit difficulty trends

## Notifications

Potential notifications include:

- Upcoming commitment
- Commitment due
- Partner checked in
- Partner needs support
- Missed commitment recovery
- Challenge milestone

Notifications should be limited and purposeful.

## Safety and moderation

The platform will eventually need:

- Reporting
- Blocking
- Privacy controls
- Abuse prevention
- Evidence handling policies
- Age-appropriate defaults
- Community moderation tools
